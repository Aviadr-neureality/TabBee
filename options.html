ℹ️ How It Works

🔍 Pattern Matching: Enter domain names (e.g., "github.com", "youtube.com") to automatically group tabs from those sites.

🪟 Multiple Windows: Tab groups are created separately for each Chrome window - this is normal Chrome behavior and keeps your workspaces organized.

🎨 Colors: Choose colors to make different groups easily identifiable at a glance.

✏️ Editing: Click "Edit" on any rule to modify the pattern, group name, or color. Click "Delete" and confirm to remove rules.
